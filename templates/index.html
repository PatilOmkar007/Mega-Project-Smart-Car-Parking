About Us

Smart Parking

We are a team of enthusiastic and driven final year students from KIT's College of Engineering in Kolhapur, passionate about solving real-world problems using cutting-edge technologies. As part of our final year project, we have successfully developed a parking occupancy detection system by combining Flask, a powerful Python web framework, and the YOLO (You Only Look Once) model for object detection. Our project aims to address the challenges associated with parking management in urban areas, including limited parking spaces, congestion, and inefficiencies. By utilizing Flask and the YOLO model, we have created an intelligent system that accurately detects and monitors the occupancy status of parking spaces in real-time.
